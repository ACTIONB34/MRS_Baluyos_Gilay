Project Overview

Features and etc

Cinema Selection Page

This page displays all the movies (and other basic info) being shown on a particular date in Cinemaworld's four cinemas. There is a link/button for each of the cinemas, which redirects to the Reservations Page when clicked.

Reservations Page

This page allows the customer to select a movie, and then it will give a list of available showings (i.e., date, cinema number & time) that the customer could choose from. - If the customer came from the Cinema Selection Page, the movie name is automatically specified, so that the customer will just select the showing he/she wants to check for reservation.

Seat Selection Page

When the customer has chosen the specific showing, the Seat Plan Page for the cinema for that particular showing is displayed. - This page also shows a table of reservations made by the customer so far. The customer may choose to cancel a particular reservation listed in this table.

Seat Plan Page

A seat plan of the cinema for the particular showing date & time is displayed (i.e., location of the cinema screen, seats per row, etc.) - The reserved seats are distinguished from the seats available for reservation. - Each of the 4 cinemas has a capacity of 72 seats only -- 9 rows of seats (labeled with the letters A to I), and having only 8 seats per row (label with digits 1 to 8). - The customer could select multiple seats to reserve before checkout. - During checkout, selected seats for reservation and the total amount due are displayed for the customer to confirm.
